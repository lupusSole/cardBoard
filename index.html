CardBoard utilizes Google's lightning fast optical character recognition technology to allow you to scan business cards into the app as interactable text far faster than you can input it manually. With 95% or greater accuracy this technology doesn't even require an internet connection!

Create

Our simple and easy to use interface allows you to create custom business cards to your specifications in a very short period of time and requires no purchase on your behalf. You can even save and print your custom cards, meaning you will never have to buy business cards again.

Share

Never be left shorthanded without a copy of your business card again. In addition, you can share your cards and cards uploaded to the app via text, email, or even social media at the touch of a button!
